Entrepreneurship Summit

E-Summit is the flagship Event of Entrepreneurship Cell MANIT, aimed at fueling the entrepreneurial spirit in the students, held start-up founders, and educating the inquisitive minds to create an innovation driven start-up culture.We aim to bring together the academic community, venture capitalists, new-age entrepreneurs and all those passionate about making a difference to common grounds and to give a chance to the students to exhibit and develop their entrepreneurial talent and creativity through various business related games, workshops, guest lectures and competitions; and to create an environment for everyone to learn and grow.

Events

Startup Expo provides a platform to connect the most disruptive startups from all over the country and investors while pitching their innovative ideas and earning valuable resources like funding, co-working space etc.

Business Plan is a contest of the best minds based on the idea of presenting a lean canvas in front of a panel of experts and pitching them the ideas.

Crypto Trading Simulation is a cluster of a workshop on gaining a deeper understanding of digital currencies and a contest of trading with the replica of numerous cryptocurrencies.

Stock Trading Simulation provides a risk-free environment with a realistic replica of the Dalal Street to try and learn relevant skills needed for trading before they trade in real life.

Hackathon is a 48-hour long event for all the coding enthusiasts to win exciting prizes by utilizing their coding skills to develop a prototype solution for real world problems.

Ipl Auction is a replica of the bidding event, challenging judgemental skills, budget management and strategizing skills to form the best possible team.

Content Conclave is a fun event aimed at introducing social media marketing tools and strategies through a series of exercises that focus on creatively promoting companies and goods.
